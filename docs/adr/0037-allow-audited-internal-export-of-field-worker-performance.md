# Allow audited internal export of field-worker performance

District leadership and service-unit supervisors need named อสม./ผรส. performance reports for follow-up meetings and training planning, but exported documents leave the access-controlled drill-down and can be circulated beyond their intended audience. The system will allow authorized supervisory roles to download or print named individual field-worker performance reports within their role scope, excluding all child-level data, logging each export event without storing a report copy, and marking every page `ใช้สำหรับการกำกับงานภายใน ห้ามเผยแพร่ต่อสาธารณะ` with reporting month, service-unit scope, creation timestamp, and report creator. In the initial release these named reports will be available as PDF only, not Excel or CSV, and only for one selected reporting month at a time; historical months may be selected individually, but multi-month accumulated performance or person-level ranking reports will not be generated. Named rows will be ordered neutrally by service unit, worker type, and name rather than by high or low performance, while retaining the numeric and status fields needed for supervisory follow-up.
