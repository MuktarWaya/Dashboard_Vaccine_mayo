# Delegate routine proactive activity management to service units

If all 14 units hold proactive sessions regularly, central creation and closure would create a bottleneck: at one session per unit per month there are at least 14 activities monthly, and at roughly weekly frequency about 56 monthly. Authorized service-unit confirmers will create and close activities for their own areas while unit staff manage targets, consent, and service results; district administrators will monitor aggregate progress and anomalies and issue aggregate reports to leadership or Pattani PAO.
