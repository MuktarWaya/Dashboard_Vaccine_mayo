# Use June as transition and start full KPI in July

As of 27 May 2569, the system still requires a new template, validation, import across 14 units, confirmed-coverage reporting, user accounts, submission workflow, locking, audit logging, and a dry run. To avoid evaluating service units on an incomplete setup, June 2569 will be designated as a transition month for real system launch, data confirmation, training, and operational observation without full completeness KPI judgment; full monthly KPI evaluation will begin in July 2569.
