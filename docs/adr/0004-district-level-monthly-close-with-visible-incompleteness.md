# Close monthly reporting centrally and disclose incomplete submissions

Monthly reporting needs a predictable closing date and a single authority while avoiding silent delays caused by missing submissions. A district-level administrator or the district vaccine program owner will close and lock the monthly report for all 14 service units by the 5th of the following month; if any unit is incomplete at close, the dashboard will display `ยังไม่ส่ง/ไม่ครบ` rather than treating missing data as zero activity or postponing closure indefinitely.
