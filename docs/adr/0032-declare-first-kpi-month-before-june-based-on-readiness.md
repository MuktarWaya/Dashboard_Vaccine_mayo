# Declare the first KPI month before June based on readiness

The original plan makes May 2569 a baseline and June the first full KPI month, but evaluating June is fair only if the real workflow is operational before June begins. By 31 May 2569, the district administrator or district vaccine program owner will notify all 14 units whether June is the first KPI month or a transition period with full KPI beginning in July, based on actual system readiness and dry-run results rather than changing conditions retrospectively.
