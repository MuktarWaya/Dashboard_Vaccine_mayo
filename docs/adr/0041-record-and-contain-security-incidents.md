# Record and contain security incidents

The system handles child-identifying data, field-worker credentials, named performance exports, and reporting evidence, so misuse or suspected disclosure must be contained without confusing it with normal data corrections. A service-unit confirmer may immediately suspend field-worker accounts in their own unit to stop an immediate risk and must notify district oversight; district administrators may temporarily suspend relevant accounts or export access, inspect audit evidence, identify potentially affected data and persons, and flag reports for review before confirmation or publication. Each security incident and response action will be recorded in a dedicated incident log separate from ordinary edit history, and notifications will follow agency data-protection guidance while avoiding unnecessary disclosure of child detail.
