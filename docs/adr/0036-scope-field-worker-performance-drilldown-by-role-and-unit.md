# Scope field-worker performance drill-down by role and unit

Performance and adoption detail for อสม./ผรส. is necessary for supervision and support, but should not be broadly exposed across service units. District leadership and district administrators may view individual drill-down across all units; service-unit heads or authorized confirmers may view individuals only within their unit; ordinary data-entry staff see only operational records required for their tasks; and field workers see only their own performance and assigned children.
