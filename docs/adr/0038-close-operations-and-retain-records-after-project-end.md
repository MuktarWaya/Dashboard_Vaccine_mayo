# Close operations and retain records after project end

The พชอ.มายอ operating period ends on 18 May 2571, but the registry, reports, and audit history contain evidence needed for accountability and may not be discarded merely because active operations end. After the end date, the system will enter a closed-for-operations state: normal activity entry and editing cease, field-worker and unnecessary operational access is suspended, and retained child registries, reports, and audit logs remain accessible only to district administrators or designated reviewers for the retention period required by applicable agency or health-record policy. Any reuse for a new project or purpose requires newly approved purpose and access authorization rather than automatic continuation of existing access.
