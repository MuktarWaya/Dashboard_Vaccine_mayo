# Let field workers report findings while service units certify official status

อสม. and ผรส. are positioned to report what occurs during household follow-up, but official vaccine status and reasons for exclusion affect district KPI and require service-unit verification. Field workers will submit follow-up date, controlled follow-up result, short note, and automatically attributed recorder through the web app; reports such as vaccinated or moved out will remain pending verification until รพ.สต./PCU staff certify any change to monthly vaccine status or registry eligibility.
