# Minimise child data shown to field workers

Field workers need enough information on a personal phone to find assigned households and carry out follow-up, but do not need full identifiers or records outside their assignments. The mobile field-work view will show only assigned children with name, age or birth date, house number, village, latest vaccine status, due follow-up month, and current-month follow-up status; full CID and PID will not be displayed, with masked CID available only when needed to distinguish duplicate names.
